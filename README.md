# Hellfied Vanilla Rebooted

## A hardcore difficulty enhancement datapack for experienced Minecraft players

Hellfied Vanilla Rebooted turns vanilla Minecraft into a relentless survival experience where hesitation is punished and mastery is rewarded.
This is not a simple difficulty tweak—it is a **hardcore evolution of vanilla combat**, built to challenge players who already know the game inside and out.

Enemies are smarter. The world is less forgiving. Every fight matters.

---

## 🎯 Overview

Hellfied Vanilla Rebooted is designed for veteran players who crave danger, tension, and meaningful combat.
By enhancing vanilla enemy AI and mechanics, the datapack creates intense encounters that demand positioning, awareness, and fast decision-making.

The core philosophy is simple:

> **Everything should feel vanilla — until it tries to kill you.**

Nothing is replaced. Everything is pushed further.

---

## ⚔️ Current Features

### Brutal Vanilla Enhancements

* Terrain and obstacles can no longer be trusted as safe zones
* Enemies relentlessly pursue players instead of giving up easily
* Combat favors skill, preparation, and smart movement
* Encounters are more dynamic, aggressive, and punishing

These changes build directly on existing vanilla systems, ensuring the experience feels authentic—just far more dangerous.

## 📥 Installation

1. Download the datapack
2. Open your world’s `datapacks` folder:

   * **Windows**: `%appdata%\.minecraft\saves\[World Name]\datapacks\`
   * **macOS**: `~/Library/Application Support/minecraft/saves/[World Name]/datapacks/`
   * **Linux**: `~/.minecraft/saves/[World Name]/datapacks/`
3. Place the datapack folder inside
4. Launch Minecraft and load the world
5. The datapack activates automatically—no setup required

### Verification

Run the following command:

```text
/datapack list
```

Ensure **Hellfied Vanilla Rebooted** appears as enabled.

## 🎮 Gameplay

Play Minecraft as usual—Hellfied mechanics activate automatically.

There are **no commands, configs, or toggles**.
Only you, the world, and significantly more dangerous enemies.

> ⚠️ **Warning**
> This datapack is intentionally unforgiving. Expect frequent deaths and high combat pressure.
